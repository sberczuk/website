Associates & Colleagues

The following sites provide tools and services that you may find useful. Most of them also have interesting general information.

  • Process Impact is a company that focuses on practical software process improvement.
  • Codefast is a product company that specializes in smart software build tools.
  • Luke Hohmann has an emphasis on creating environments where everyone can work to their fullest creative and intellectual abilities.
  • Linda Rising is an independent consultant who can help you with patterns, project retrospectives, and introducing new ideas into your organization.
  • Dave Thomas and Andy Hunt, the Pragmatic Programmers have some great resources on their web site. Their pragmatic approach to software development is very much in alignment with the ideas in the SCM Patterns book.
