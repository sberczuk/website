Arlington & Vicinity

Some useful Arlington links follow. If you need to find your way around the town, you might want to take a look at this map of Arlington.

  • The Official Town Web site. This site contains answers to some Frequently Asked Questions for new residents.
  • Exclusively Arlington has an assortment of information about about Arlington.
  • Paul Schlichtman's community site has a good amount of information about the town, including politically oriented links and links to resident's personal pages.
  • The Arlington List; this site also contains recommendations about local businesses from list members. There are also many other Arlington related Discussion Lists that residents may want to sign up for.
  • The Arlington Advocate (the local newspaper) online version.
  • Fitness First, the excellent local gym that I am a member of.
  • The Arlington Friends of the Drama, a community theatre group.
  • The Regent Theatre.
  • The FEI Theatres the group that owns the Capitol and Somerville theatres, two excellent second-run theatres in the area.
